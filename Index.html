Purpose

This document outlines the standard operating rhythm for the global business development organization at Simpro, ensuring consistent execution, communication, and performance management at all levels of the org. It ensures that the BDR leaders whose teams focus on identifying, qualifying potential leads, generating new opportunities, and collaborating with account executives have clear operational guidance to run their teams effectively in Simpro.

Value Creation Levers

Our strategy is built on five core pillars. These levers outline the key activities and systems that drive our pipeline generation and commercial success.

1. Performance & Data

  • KPI Tracking: Daily monitoring of plan vs. actuals for MQLs, call and email activities, meeting bookings, conversion rates, and pipeline creation via both Inbound and Outbound motions.
  • Stack Rankings: Monthly and quarterly rankings on BDR performance to drive accountability and recognize success.
  • Active Slack Channels: Celebrate every SQL for instant recognition of good work to keep morale high and encourage performance.

2. Systems & Tools

  • CRM Hygiene: Consistent and accurate updates in the CRM on Leads and Opportunities are mandatory for reliable forecasting and handoff to sales.
  • Sales Engagement: Utilize SE tools like Hubspot, Nooks, Sendoso, and Sakari in conjunction with SFDC to drive prospect engagement and create meetings.
  • AI Support: Use AI for persona research and assisted messaging to accelerate outbound.

3. Talent & Enablement

  • Coaching & Development: Structured one-on-one sessions and call coaching to build skills and support career progression.
  • Recruiting & Onboarding: Maintain a high bar for talent, ensuring all new hires have our ideal BDR traits of Curiosity, Enthusiasm, and Grit. Ramp new hires effectively with product, industry, and on-the-job training.
  • Continual Training: Regular enablement sessions on outbound skills, AI augmentation, product, and contracting workflows to effectively communicate Simproâ€™s value.

4. Go-to-Market Execution (BDR-Aligned)

  • Lead Management & Conversion: Enforce SLAs for MQL and MQA follow-up and track conversion rates to optimize workflows.
  • AE & Marketing Alignment: Drive territory-level alignment with Account Executives and provide structured feedback to Marketing on lead quality and campaigns.
  • Outbound Execution: Partner with the OBCOE to launch targeted campaigns and operationalize account prioritization frameworks.

5. Continuous Improvement (BDR-Aligned)

  • Performance Reviews: Conduct monthly retros and structured campaign reviews to analyze performance, identify blockers, and refine strategy.
  • Playbook & Process Refinement: Maintain and iterate on qualification frameworks and playbooks based on real-time feedback and new tactics.
  • Coaching & Learning: Implement regular call reviews, peer coaching, and deal huddles to drive skill development and share best practices.
  • Enablement Feedback Loop: Serve as frontline sensors to capture rep feedback on tooling, content, and enablement effectiveness.
